Getting Started

How We're Planning This

Starting small and simple. We want to build something sustainable that actually works for busy founders, investors, and mentors.

1

Sign Up

Let us know you're interested and a bit about your background. We'll keep group sizes small and try to balance the mix of roles.

2

Get Invited

We'll send details for upcoming sessions. Planning bi-weekly meetups with 12-16 people per session.

3

Play & Connect

3-hour evening sessions with 4-5 rounds of chess. Rotating partners ensure you meet everyone. Light refreshments provided.

4

Stay Connected

Keep the conversations going. We'll set up ways for the group to stay in touch between sessions.
